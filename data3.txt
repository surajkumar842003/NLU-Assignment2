Biography

Sumit Kalra is an Associate Professor in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at the Indian Institute of Technology Jodhpur. His work bridges smart healthcare, cloud-native software, and human-centered technology to deliver scalable, real-world solutions.

He also serves as Program Committee Member for MedTech Center and contributes to program management teams driving medical technology initiatives with AIIMS Jodhpur.
